GRAVITY SWITCHER

PUZZLE PLATFORMER

PLAY ON ITCH.IO

THE DIRECTIVE

Most puzzle games hold your hand. Gravity Switcher exists to break your brain. It is a spatial puzzle platformer that weaponizes perspective against the player. The core mechanic isn't jumping or shooting; it's total environmental manipulation—violently shifting the gravitational axis to bend the architecture of the level to your will.

THE PIVOT

I wanted to strip away cheap, traditional lock-and-key tropes. I despise arbitrary barriers. The goal was to build a space where the only key required to solve the puzzle is the player's ability to forcefully reorient their mental map of physical space. You don't unlock the door; you drop the ceiling until the door becomes the floor.

THE ENGINE OF CHAOS

  • Architectural Subversion: Shift gravitational pull to the walls or ceiling, instantly transforming a safe floorplan into a lethal hazard.
  • Cognitive Load: Forces the player to constantly shatter and rebuild their understanding of up, down, and momentum.

THE AUTOPSY

The Blood: Implementing a global gravity shift demands ruthless precision in the physics engine. A single micro-glitch in collision detection ruins the illusion. When tuned perfectly, the mechanic forcefully ejects the player from their comfort zone and generates those brutal, satisfying "Aha!" moments by snapping their perspective in half.
